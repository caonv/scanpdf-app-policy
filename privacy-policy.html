Privacy Policy - Scan PDF Pro

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

1. Overview

Scan PDF Pro ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

2. Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data:

  • No account or login required
  • No backend server
  • No documents sent anywhere
  • No user activity tracking

3. Image Processing

All scanned images are processed 100% on your device. You control when to delete data.

4. Permissions

The App requests:

  • Camera: To scan documents
  • Photo Library: To select existing images
  • Device Storage: To save PDF files

5. Security

Since processing happens on your device only, your data never leaves your device.
